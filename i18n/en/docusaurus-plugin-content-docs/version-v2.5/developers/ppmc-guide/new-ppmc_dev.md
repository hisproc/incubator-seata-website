# New PPMC Member Guide
This document primarily serves as a guide for new PPMC members to Apache Seata™ (incubating).

Upon receiving an invitation email from Seata PPMC, a new PPMC member should consider whether accepting. If they decide in favor, they should select "Reply All" and express their decision.

## Subscribe to Private Mailing List

1. Send email to [private-subscribe@seata.apache.org](mailto:private-subscribe@seata.apache.org)
2. You will receive an email with the subject "confirm subscribe to private@seata.apache.org"
3. Reply to the email with "Confirm" in the body

If you receive an email with the subject "WELCOME to private@seata.apache.org", you have successfully subscribed to the private mailing list.

PPMC members are required to subscribe to the private@seata.apache.org mailing list, which is restricted to confidential discussions involving embargoed security vulnerability, or other sensitive matters that must not be publicly disclosed. This aligns with ASF’s Security Handling [Guidelines](https://www.apache.org/security/).

## Read PMC Member Guide

Please read [PMC GUIDE](https://www.apache.org/dev/pmc.html) to know about the general responsibilities of Project Management Committee (PMC) members in managing our project and common how-to procedures for day to day maintenance.
